# 🛡️ Refund & Return Policy

This policy governs all transactions on **Naproxen™ The Market**. Please read these terms carefully before completing a purchase. By buying or selling on our platform, you agree to these terms.

---

## 🟢 When Refunds Are Allowed

A buyer may request a refund under the following strict conditions:
* **Defective Products:** The digital code, key, or item does not work or is defective upon arrival.
* **Misrepresentation:** The item received does not match the description or attributes provided in the marketplace listing.
* **Physical Item Agreements:** For physical goods, a return is allowed *only* if the seller explicitly agrees to it, and the buyer opens an official dispute while the transaction is still ongoing.

---

## 📋 Return Guidelines by Category

### 💾 Digital Items & Game Keys
Once a digital item or game key has been delivered and received by the buyer, **no returns or refunds are permitted** under any normal circumstances due to the nature of digital goods.

### 🤖 Escrowed Items (Bot Trades)
Once an item has been successfully delivered via bot trade, **no returns are allowed**. 
* All escrowed items are delivered in the exact condition they are listed; therefore, they cannot be misrepresented. 
* Delivered escrowed items **do not** fall under the *Naproxen™ Buyer Guarantee*.

### 📦 Physical Items
Once a physical item has been delivered, there are no returns **unless the Seller specifically agrees to one**. 
* If the seller agrees to a return, you must immediately open a dispute while the transaction is still active.
* You must work directly with the seller to coordinate the return shipment.

> ⚠️ **Note:** Naproxen™ The Market does not provide return shipping labels, nor do we cover or reimburse any shipping costs for returned items.

---

## 🚫 Fraudulent Claims & Chargebacks

Fraudulent claims harm our community ecosystem and are **strictly prohibited**. Fraudulent behavior includes, but is not limited to:
1. A buyer filing a chargeback or dispute via their payment processor *after* successfully receiving their purchased item.
2. A buyer filing a payment chargeback *after* explicitly confirming and completing the transaction on our platform.

---

## ⚖️ Policy Enforcement

* **Immediate Penalties:** Accounts or user profiles associated with fraudulent claims, chargebacks, or friendly fraud will face immediate penalties.
* **Permanent Bans:** Naproxen™ The Market reserves the right to permanently ban any user who violates this return policy or attempts to defraud a seller.

> 💡 **What to do instead:** If an item does not match its description, **do not open a chargeback with your bank**. Please open a support dispute on our server while the transaction is still ongoing, or reach out directly to our staff for assistance.
