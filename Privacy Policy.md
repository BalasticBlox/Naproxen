# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated:** June 14, 2026
Thank you for choosing **StockPulse™** ("the Bot"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with the Bot, our Discord support server, or related services. By adding the Bot to your server or interacting with it, you agree to the data practices described in this policy.
## 1. Information We Collect
To function properly, StockPulse™ collects limited and specific data provided directly via the Discord API or through your interactions with the Bot. This data includes:
 * **User Information:** Discord User IDs, usernames, and avatars.
 * **Server Information:** Server (Guild) IDs, channel IDs, channel names, and role IDs.
 * **Bot Activity Data:** Command usage history, custom configuration configurations (e.g., prefix settings, server-specific features), and simulation or internal economy data.
 * **Support Data:** Any information you provide voluntarily when contacting us via email or opening a support ticket in our Discord server.
We **do not** log or read your private personal messages, and we do not collect sensitive real-world personal information (such as phone numbers, physical addresses, or real-world financial details).
## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data strictly to operate, maintain, and improve the Bot. Specifically, for:
 * Executing commands and managing server-specific settings.
 * Saving game/simulation progress, user profiles, or configuration states.
 * Identifying and fixing bugs, preventing abuse, or stopping malicious exploit attempts.
 * Providing customer support when you reach out to us.
## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties
Your data privacy is paramount to us.
 * **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third-party companies or advertisers.
 * **Legal Disclosures:** We will only share data if strictly required by law, court order, or to enforce our Terms of Service in the event of severe abuse or legal violations.
## 4. Data Retention and Deletion
 * **Retention:** We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the Bot's services. If a server kicks or removes the Bot, its configuration data may remain in our cache/database for a short period before being automatically or manually pruned.
 * **Your Rights (Data Deletion Request):** You have full control over your data. If you wish to have your user data or your server's data permanently deleted from our database, please contact us via our **Support Server** or **Email** (listed below). We will process your deletion request within 30 days.
## 5. Security
We take reasonable measures to secure your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
## 6. Children's Privacy
StockPulse™ complies with Discord's Terms of Service. The Bot is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a user under 13, we will delete it immediately.
## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When updates occur, we will change the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be announced in our official support server.
