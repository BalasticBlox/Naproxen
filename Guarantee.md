# 🛡️ The Naproxen™ Buyer Guarantee

All purchases made within our marketplace are automatically protected by the **Naproxen™ Guarantee**. This ensures that you either receive your item exactly as described or you get your money back.

---

## 🟢 When You’re Covered

You can open an official claim under the Naproxen™ Guarantee if:
* ❌ **Non-Delivery:** You do not receive the item you paid for.
* ⚠️ **Defective Item:** The item you receive does not work or is defective.
* 📝 **Misrepresentation:** The item does not match the description provided in the marketplace listing.
* 🎮 **Unfulfilled Services:** A service session (such as boosting or coaching) you booked was not delivered.

---

## 🛑 What’s Not Covered

The Guarantee **does not apply** if:
* You complete and rate the transaction despite having problems with the item.
* You simply change your mind (*buyer’s remorse*).
* The item is damaged or lost when you arrange your own shipping or pick-up (for physical items).
* The item was delivered via bot trade (escrowed), as these are automated to match the listing exactly.
* The transaction took place outside of Naproxen™ platforms or servers, which is strictly prohibited by our Terms of Use.

---

## 📋 How to File a Claim

If you experience an issue with your purchase, follow these steps immediately:

1. **Contact the Seller:** First, message the seller directly to see if the issue can be resolved quickly and amicably.
2. **Open a Dispute:** If the seller is uncooperative, notify our staff immediately to open an official dispute. **This will pause the standard 3-day automatic transaction completion countdown.**
3. **Provide Evidence:** Share screenshots, video recordings, or any relevant proof of the issue with the staff.
4. **Staff Review:** The Naproxen™ Support team will review your case. If your claim qualifies, we will authorize a refund to your original payment method.

---

## ⚖️ Important Rules

* **⏳ 3-Day Window:** You have exactly **3 days** from the time the item is marked as delivered/received to open an official dispute.
* **⏱️ Response Deadline:** If staff requests more information or evidence regarding your claim, you must reply within **48 hours** or the claim may be automatically denied.
* **🚫 Fraud Prevention:** Fraudulent claims (such as filing a chargeback after successfully receiving an item) will result in an immediate and permanent ban from the marketplace.
* **🔨 Final Decision:** Both buyers and sellers agree that the Naproxen™ Support team's decision is final, including any subsequent appeals.
* **🔒 Privacy Note:** We reserve the right to provide buyers and sellers with access to relevant contact information or transaction details if necessary to resolve a dispute.

---

> ℹ️ **Note:** The Naproxen™ Guarantee is a marketplace safety policy and does not act as a long-term product warranty.
